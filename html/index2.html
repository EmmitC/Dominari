Featured Author
Jasper Okedi

"THREADS OF TIME: SOME DAY OUR LIGHT WILL GIVE OUT"

Any man or woman will greatly benefit when they pick up this book to gain insight into the pure and true intimacy between Akim/a man restored to God (As the Great Shepherd King was) and Akipi/a woman restored to God (As a godly feminine maiden is).

"My writing is meant to achieve the above using “dialogue poetry”, to lead both men and women into their true roles. The man embraces the masculine while the woman embraces the feminine. The man leads the woman while she submits. This is the proper way of relationship function."
Jasper Okedi, Author
